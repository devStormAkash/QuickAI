## 💡 Overview

**QuickAI** is a cutting-edge, full-stack **SaaS platform** that centralizes powerful AI functionalities into one sleek application. From generating captivating blog content to performing precise image edits and reviewing your resume, QuickAI is designed to boost your productivity and creativity.

It's built with a modern tech stack, ensuring high performance, scalability, and a smooth user experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

QuickAI offers a suite of high-value, AI-powered tools:

### Content Generation
* ✍️ **Article & Blog Title Generation:** Instantly create compelling, SEO-friendly content and titles.
* ✅ **Resume Review:** Get AI feedback on your resume structure, content, and phrasing.

### Creative Imaging
* 🖼️ **AI Image Generation:** Turn text prompts into stunning visual art.
* ✂️ **Background Removal:** Cleanly isolate subjects with a single click.
* 🚫 **Object Removal:** Seamlessly erase unwanted objects from photos.

### Platform Architecture
* 🔒 **Secure Authentication:** Managed entirely by **Clerk** for quick and secure sign-up/login.
* 💳 **Integrated Billing:** Subscription tiers and payment processing powered by **Clerk**.
